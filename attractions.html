Notable Places

Barangay Canitoan's facilities, subdivisions, and institutions

Community Center

Barangay Hall

The administrative center of Barangay Canitoan, situated in Zone 2 Canitoan. It serves as the primary office for public documents processing, local resolution hearings, and general citizens support services.

📍 Zone 2 Canitoan

Notable Locations

A blend of municipal facilities and community institutions.

Barangay Hall

Central government headquarters in Zone 2 for local documents, clearances, and resident certification services.

📍 Zone 2 Canitoan

Barangay Health Center

Main local healthcare unit providing routine child immunization, maternal check-ups, and essential medicine distribution.

📍 Zone 4 Canitoan

Barangay Covered Court

Multi-purpose indoor community gym hosting assemblies, sports tournaments, and annual socio-cultural events.

📍 Zone 2 Canitoan

Sacred Heart of Jesus Parish

A major religious parish in Canitoan, serving as a spiritual sanctuary and hosting local outreach programs.

📍 Zone 2 Canitoan near Barangay Hall

Canitoan National High School

The primary secondary educational institution in the barangay, providing quality education and student leadership programs.

📍 Zone 1 Canitoan

Dear Joe Coffee & Juice

A popular local café offering a variety of coffee, tea, and refreshing juices in a cozy atmosphere.

📍 Midtown

L.A.D. Family Resort

A budget-friendly private getaway in Agong, Canitoan, featuring swimming pools, cottages, and overnight rooms tailored for exclusive family gatherings, team buildings, and private celebrations.

📍 District 4 Agong

Casa de Canitoan

It features a large white main building, beautifully manicured garden grounds, an outdoor swimming pool, and full bed-and-breakfast accommodations.

📍 Zone 2 Macapagal Drive

Casa Cafe

It provides a cozy, relaxed indoor dining space alongside its newer greenhouse extension, Casa Verde, which immerses diners in lush greenery and natural glasshouse lighting.

📍 Zone 2 Canitoan-Pagatpat Road.

Visitor Guide

How to Get Here

  • Located about 6 kilometers from Cagayan de Oro City Proper
  • Takes approximately 15–20 minutes by public transportation
  • Regular jeepney and taxi routes connect the barangay to major commercial centers
  • Tricycles serve local transport needs across all 10 zones

Community Reminders

  • Respect local environment rules — segregate waste in public spaces
  • Coordinate school or center visits with administrative staff at the Barangay Hall
  • Help maintain peace and safety — report concerns to Barangay Tanods
  • Support local neighborhood enterprises and farm produce vendors
