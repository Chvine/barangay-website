Celebrate with Us

Festivals & Events

A year-round calendar of cultural celebrations and community milestones

Main Celebration

Barangay Fiesta

Our grandest annual celebration of faith, culture, and community unity in Barangay Canitoan.

Faith & Culture

Fiesta Celebration

Residents participate in various religious, cultural, sports, and social activities that strengthen community ties and showcase local talents.

Typical Fiesta Activities

• Thanksgiving Mass
• Civic Parade
• Sports Competitions
• Cultural Presentations
• Community Night
• Talent Competitions
• Food Fair
• Youth Activities & Awards
Calendar of Events

Annual Events Calendar

Mark your calendar and join us in celebrating these meaningful occasions throughout the year.

23Jan
Religious

Feast of San Juan Kapistrano (Pre-Celebration)

Novena masses, street decorating competitions, and community merienda cena to begin the Kapistahan season.

14Feb
Cultural

Hearts & Homes: Barangay Valentine Fair

A community bazaar featuring local food, crafts, and live entertainment celebrating love and togetherness.

15Mar
Civic

Linis Barangay Day

Annual community clean-up drive covering all 12 puroks, with tree-planting activities and environmental awareness campaigns.

HolyWeek
Religious

Semana Santa Observances

Traditional Stations of the Cross, Salubong, Pabasa ng Pasyon, and the Easter Sunday Mass and celebration.

1May
Cultural

Flores de Mayo / Santacruzan

Month-long flower offerings to the Blessed Virgin Mary, culminating in the grand Santacruzan procession on the last Sunday of May.

12Jun
🇵🇭 Civic

Independence Day Celebration

Flag ceremony, patriotic performances by local students, essay contests, and a community program highlighting Filipino heritage.

20Aug
Fiesta

Barangay Founding Anniversary

Commemorating the founding of Barangay Canitoan in 1952 with a historical exhibit, talent show, sports competitions, and recognition of outstanding citizens.

BuwanAug
🇵🇭 Cultural

Buwan ng Wika Celebration

Month-long celebration of the Filipino language with poster-making contests, debates, story-telling, and a grand closing program.

23Oct
Grand Fiesta

Kapistahan ni San Juan Kapistrano

The biggest event of the year! Three days of processions, cultural shows, street dancing, beauty pageants, sports tournaments, live concerts, and fireworks.

1–2Nov
Religious

Undas (All Saints'/Souls' Day)

Community gathering at the local cemetery for prayers, candle-lighting, and honoring departed loved ones with the traditional Filipino spirit of malasakit.

16Dec
Christmas

Simbang Gabi & Pasko Celebration

Nine-day dawn masses, Lantern Festival, Christmas caroling competition, Noche Buena communal dinner, and the grand Media Noche fireworks.

SKYear-Round
Youth

SK Youth Programs

Quarterly SK-organized events: Youth Leadership Camp, Sports Fest, Environmental Clean-up, and Livelihood Skills Training for barangay youth aged 15–24.
