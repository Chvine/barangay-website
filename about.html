About Barangay Canitoan

Profile, subdivisions, mission, and official seal explanation

Overview

Barangay Profile

Barangay Canitoan is situated in the western part of Cagayan de Oro City. It is characterized by a combination of residential communities, commercial developments, agricultural lands, and institutional establishments.

Continuous infrastructure development has transformed the barangay into one of the city's major growth areas while maintaining its commitment to environmental protection and community welfare.

Organization

Political Subdivisions (Zones)

Barangay Canitoan is divided into ten (10) zones. Click cards to activate highlight.

Zone 1

Ilaya & Tangke

Traditional residential sectors located in the core parts of the community.

Zone 2

Centro, GSIS & Isla

Includes the Centro area, GSIS Subdivision, Isla, and Manticao Drive.

Zone 3

Scions & Macapaya

Phase 1 & Phase 2 Scions Subdivision and Macapaya residential area.

Zone 4

Looc, Agong & Hillside

Includes Sitios Looc, Agong, and the surrounding Hillside communities.

Zone 5

Relocation Site & Reyes

Phase 1, Phase 2A and B Relocation Site, and Reyes Compound.

Zone 6

Calaanan & Springside

Sitios Calaanan, Puli, and the Springside residential area.

Zone 7

GSIS Housing & Flores

Old PN Roa Road, GSIS Housing, and Flores Compound.

Zone 8

Upper Canitoan

The elevated highland sector of the barangay with scenic views.

Zone 9

PN Roa Subdivision

Major residential housing subdivision for families.

Zone 10

Oro Habitat & Relocations

Oro Habitat, Mahogany Village, Talongan Village, Quadruplex, and nearby relocation communities.

Purpose

Vision & Mission

Vision

A progressive and resilient barangay where healthy and disciplined people live.

Mission

The Barangay Government of Canitoan is committed to:

  • • Delivering efficient, transparent, and responsive public service.
  • • Promoting peace, security, and social order.
  • • Strengthening disaster preparedness and community resilience.
  • • Protecting the environment through sustainable programs.
  • • Supporting education, health, youth, senior citizens, women, and persons with disabilities.
  • • Encouraging active citizen participation in governance.

Core Values

Integrity

We serve our citizens with honesty, transparency, and high moral standards in all public services.

Accountability

We take full responsibility for our actions, administrative decisions, policies, and community resources.

Transparency

We keep public records, official transactions, and barangay council decisions open and accessible to all residents.

Excellence

We strive to deliver the highest quality of service, administrative efficiency, and prompt public response.

Unity

We work hand-in-hand with all zones, subdivisions, and local sectors to build a cohesive community.

Respect

We honor the diverse voices, beliefs, cultural backgrounds, and rights of every Canitoan citizen.

Compassion

We prioritize the welfare of vulnerable sectors, providing social assistance, care, and active outreach.

Stewardship

We actively protect and conserve our environment, local resources, and public lands for future generations.
