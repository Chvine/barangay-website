What We Offer

Barangay Frontline Services

Barangay Canitoan provides a wide range of services to meet the needs of every resident.

📜

Barangay Certificates & Clearances

Processing of Barangay Clearance, Barangay Certification, Certificate of Residency, Certificate of Indigency, Business Clearance, and Business Permit Endorsements.

📋

Blotter, Mediation & Justice

Documentation of local incidents, Barangay Blotter requests, and Sangguniang mediation hearings through the Katarungang Pambarangay system.

👵

Social Welfare Assistance

Coordination and local filing support for Senior Citizens registration, Solo Parent benefits, and Persons with Disabilities (PWD) social assistance.

💊

Health Programs

Primary family care, immunization, medical missions, child nutrition campaigns, and regular vaccination updates at the Health Center.

🚨

Disaster Response

Emergency assistance coordination, evacuation center setups, relief distribution programs, and quick incident report actions.

🌿

Environmental Programs

Waste management education campaigns, community clean-up drives, tree-planting programs, and neighborhood greening initiatives.

⚽

Youth & Sports Development

Sangguniang Kabataan initiatives, physical fitness camps, inter-zone sports leagues, and leadership development workshops.

💼

Livelihood & Community Governance

Vocational skills trainings, community general assembly coordination, and broadcasting of public information announcements.

📋 General Requirements for Barangay Documents

  • At least one (1) valid government-issued ID (driver's license, passport, PhilSys, UMID, Voter's ID, Senior ID)
  • Completed request form (available at the Barangay Hall lobby)
  • Small documentary processing fee (varies by document type, typically ₱20–₱50)
  • For clearances: must have no pending barangay-level cases or active blotter records

Find Us

Location Map

The Barangay Hall is located in Zone 2 Centro, easily accessible from the main CDO highway.

Map source: OpenStreetMap contributors. View larger map

🚌 By Public Transport

  • Ride a jeepney from CDO town proper heading to Barangay Canitoan
  • Trip schedule: 6 AM to 9 PM daily
  • Travel time: approximately 15–20 minutes

🛺 By Local Tricycle

  • Tricycles are readily available at the entrance terminal
  • Serves all local zones including relocation sites
  • Standard fare rates apply across zones

🚗 By Private Vehicle

  • From CDO highway: Turn at the Canitoan crossing sign
  • Drive approximately 6 kilometers directly along the paved road
  • The Barangay Hall is clearly marked in Zone 2 Centro
